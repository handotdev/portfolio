Wiema Dental

Wiema was founded in 2011, originally as Dentware. The company specializes in hi-tech customized dental devices such as implant-borne crowns and bridgeworks in ceramic, cobalt chrome and titanium. The products are carefully designed and manufactured in Helsingborg, Sweden and shipped all over Europe.
